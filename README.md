> A curated list of platforms, tools, resources, communities, methodologies, and learning materials for self-directed learning.

Self-learning has never been more accessible. With open educational resources, online courses, digital libraries, AI-powered learning tools, and global communities, individuals can develop new skills and deepen their knowledge across virtually any subject. Effective self-learning combines curiosity, discipline, critical thinking, and lifelong learning habits.

This list is intended for students, professionals, researchers, educators, career changers, lifelong learners, and anyone interested in learning independently.

## Learning Platforms

Platforms that provide educational content across a wide range of subjects.

- [Khan Academy](https://www.khanacademy.org/) — Free educational platform covering mathematics, science, economics, and more.
- [Coursera](https://www.coursera.org/) — Online courses and professional certificates from universities and organizations.
- [edX](https://www.edx.org/) — University-level courses from institutions around the world.
- [FutureLearn](https://www.futurelearn.com/) — Online learning platform offering courses and microcredentials.
- [LinkedIn Learning](https://www.linkedin.com/learning/) — Professional skills and career-focused learning platform.
- [Udemy](https://www.udemy.com/) — Marketplace of courses covering technology, business, creativity, and more.

## Open Educational Resources

## Study Techniques & Methodologies

Frameworks and approaches for improving learning effectiveness.

- [Spaced Repetition](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spaced_repetition) — Learning technique for long-term retention.
- [Active Recall](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Testing_effect) — Learning through retrieval practice.
- [Feynman Technique](https://fs.blog/feynman-technique/) — Method for understanding concepts through explanation.
- [Pomodoro Technique](https://francescocirillo.com/pages/pomodoro-technique) — Time management method for focused learning.
- [Interleaving](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interleaving_(education)) — Learning strategy involving mixed practice.
- [Deliberate Practice](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deliberate_practice) — Structured approach to skill development.

## Learning Resources

Books, guides, and resources focused on learning how to learn.

- [Learning How to Learn](https://www.coursera.org/learn/learning-how-to-learn) — Popular course on effective learning strategies.
- [Ultralearning](https://www.scottyoung.com/blog/ultralearning/) — Framework for intensive self-directed learning.
- [Make It Stick](https://www.hup.harvard.edu/books/9780674729018) — Research-based guide to effective learning.
- [The First 20 Hours](https://first20hours.com/) — Guide to rapid skill acquisition.
- [How to Take Smart Notes](https://takesmartnotes.com/) — Practical guide to the Zettelkasten method.
- [The Art of Learning](https://www.joshwaitzkin.com/the-art-of-learning) — Book on performance and learning mastery.
